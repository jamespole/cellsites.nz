Cell Sites in Aotearoa

Welcome to my collection of cell site photos. I have been taking photos of cell sites since 2003. My interest was sparked by my natural curiosity in how things work. I wanted to find out how mobile phones worked.

I discovered that other people had their own collections of cell site photos. So I decided I would create my own. There are also photos of other wireless and/or telco sites.
